Tips for organisation

  • Use reminders, schedules and to-do lists
  • Use colors while taking notes, and possibly do it in a quiet environment
  • Try the pomodoro technique
  • Use specific names when sorting files

Tips for those who suffer from depression

  • Adopt a pet and take care of it
  • Aim for 8 hours of sleep each nigth
  • Try engaging in more social events
  • Start playing a sport, keep your body moving!
  • Develop an hobby such as gardening

Tips for those suffering from eating disorders

  • For how hard this might sound, stop counting calories, delete those apps
  • Try mindful eating, listen to your body: if you're hungry eat, even if it feels hard
  • Don't keep on eating after you start feeling full, it could induce a binge
  • If you see someone suffer from such disease stay as close as possible to them, and if you are the one who's suffering seek for help from either doctors or your loved ones

Tips for those who suffer from (social) anxiety

  • Keep a journal of how you feel each day
  • Talk to who you trust
  • Develop an hobby that calms you
  • This is very difficult, but try to engage in more social activities: start maybe going out with your close friends or family members
  • If you worry a lot about your body and face image, or in general how you look, avoid mirrors
