Binge Eating Disorder (BED)

What is BED?

Binge eating, instead of being an eating disorder that aims for thinness and so implies self-destructive behavior, is a disorder characterized by the frequent consumation of excessive quantities of food without any self control. People who suffer from BED feel ashamed of their binges and vow to stop, but cannot resist the urges and so the loop repeats. It appears that those who suffer from it do not value too much their weight and body image, and so do not make themselves purge or excessively workout right after.

What are its symptoms and effects?

Overeating frequently, until uncomfortably full | Prefering to eat alone or in secret | Feeling out of control when eating | Feeling ashamed, disgusted and guilty after a binge
