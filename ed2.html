BULIMIA NERVOSA

What is bulimia?

Bulimic people eat from hundreds to thousands of calories without any control, until they feel uncomfortably full ( this can happen either in secret or not), and afterwards, due to guilty feelings they purge (over exercising, or self-induged vomiting, to get rid of the extra calories. Bulimic people are also obsessed with their weight, body and shape, they base their self-worth on it. Same as anorexic people, those who are affected by bulimia also are more likely to refuse treatment at first.

What causes it?

The causes are the same as anorexia: beauty standards and bullying.

What are its symptoms and effects?

Living in fear of gaining weight | Body dysmorphia, distorted vision of own body | Self-induced purging after meals | Social withdrawal | Lack of control during binges | Teeth rotting | Insomnia | Sore throat | Fainting, dizziness | Irregular period | Calluses and swollen glands | Dangerous weight gain and loss | Always feeling cold
