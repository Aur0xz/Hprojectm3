DEPRESSION

Depression is a very serious mental illness that negatively affects how you feel, how you think and how you act; it mainly causes sadness, decreased energy, and loss of interest in activities you used to enjoy, in the worst cases depression can cause suicidal thoughts, self-harm and suicide. People of all ages, genders and ethnicities can suffer from it, teenagers and young adults are most likely to suffer from it. It has been proved that 1 person in 6 suffers or has suffered from depression.
